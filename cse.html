Computer Science

 




What is Computer Science?

As a freshmen one of the first engineering classes that is taken is Computer Science Engineering. Although it may sound intimidating at first, but with the excellent teaching done by Mr. Wood, students are able to expand and develop their knowledge in various computer languages. One of the basic languages introduced as freshmen is Python, which is taught at an introductory level, but understanding these concepts will lead to the creation of larger and more complex programs. Later on with the use of MIT programs such as "Scratch" and "Alice", students are able to create animated games and storylines as one of their projects.



Instructor: Mr. Wood

What will I learn after I take Computer Science?

After taking the class students will have the ability to:

  • Write complex programs in Java
  • Understand the basic syntax of a computer language
  • Master the art of animation through the use of Alice
  • Develop multiple games with the storyline process
  • Use the Software Development Process
