Principles of Engineering

 




What is Principles of Engineering?

Principles of Engineering is a course that really gives students the feel of what it's really like to be an engineer. This course deals mostly with mechanical engineering. At the beginning of the course, students start with learning the basics such as how certain simple machines work or how to manipulate multiple simple machines to work together. Every time students master a concept, they get to build a prototype of it. This can range anywhere from simple levers to complex trusses. This course teaches critical thinking as well as hands on building.



Instructors: Mr. McGee, Mr. Valdez

What will I learn after I take Principles of Engineering

After taking the class students will have the ability to:

  • Knowledge of mechanical engineering
  • Mathematical application with simple magazines
  • Efficient use and construction of mechanical parts
  • Integrate software with hardware
  • Be exposed to tools that can be applied to any other engineering field
