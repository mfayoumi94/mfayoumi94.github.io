Civil Engineering and Architecture

 




What is Civil Engineering and Architecture?

The world of architectural design surrounds us everywhere. The student who take this class will be better prepared to understand how residential and commercial designs influence the way we live and work. CEA is the study of building design and civil engineering concepts. This study includes the home and workplace with emphasis on landscape design. Students in this class will use advanced computer aided design and structural simulation software. By combining these study areas and skills the student will be well prepared to decide if architectural design or civil engineering is a valid future career choice.



Instructor: Mr. Coalson

What will I learn after I take Civil Engineering and Architecture?

After taking the class students will have the ability to:

  • Effectively use Revit Architecture
  • Understand the fundamentals of structural design
  • Improve public speaking upon presenting designs
  • Use landscape equipment to take measurements
  • Apply construction techniques efficiently
