Service

TSA, establishing our commitment to service, ran our annual canned food drive- supporting our local food bank. As a part of our TSA chapter, we strive to impact our societies beyond the classrooms, labs, and computers. Living in South Texas, one of the most impoverished communities in our nation, giving back to our community allows high school students to interact with everyday members of the community.
