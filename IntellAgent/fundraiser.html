Fundraisers

Our chapter has reached out with several organizations to create multiple fundraisers and events in order to raise money for our transportation, and other specific accommodations. For example, just last semester the Science Academy's TSA chapter sold tickets to both the student body, and public in hopes that one lucky person would win an iPad 2 Air. A special congratulations to Ms. Rodriguez for being the winner of this fundraiser.



Competition

The Science Academy of South Texas is very competitive, strives to persevere, and--most importantly--maintains honesty at all times. Since our TSA chapter was created, we have ensured that members begin events with sufficient time for them to complete and give their projects their utmost attention. As a result, last year multiple teams placed in regional, state, and national competitions. This year we have maintained the standards our chapter holds dear and, as a result, our school continues to uphold itself so that students reach and surpass the high ambitions that define the very spirit they use to achieve greatness.
