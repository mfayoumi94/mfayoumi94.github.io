Our Software

How does it work?

    Our iPhone application uses a variety of databases in order to identify words in speech. By detecting words and transcribing them into text, IntellAgent is able to run the divisibles through a machine learning algorithm with over 90 layers. By applying these constraints to each word, we are able to identify its sign-language counterpart and display it on the screen. All of this is done within .3 seconds, allowing for a smooth, fast, and easy learning process in the classroom. Now, hearing impairments can never be an excuse to avoid an education.

    We also developed a hardware device that utilizes the same software but doesn't require an iPhone. Therefore, even if you're in a low-income area or country, you can still learn with ease!



Benefits

 

    By using this application, you will be able to learn in any school or university without the need for a translator. By having the independence that everyone deserves, your access to education will finally be available.

Now, instead of being limited to studying at Gallaudet University, a world-renowned deaf university, you can instead study at any school of your choosing. Just turn on your iPhone or hardware device and watch your lecture!
