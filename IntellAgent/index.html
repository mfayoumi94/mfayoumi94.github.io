About Us

Who are we?
 
We are a robotics company using artificial intelligence to create assistive technology for students who have hearing impairments.

Our Mission

To empower the future leaders of tomorrow by leveraging robotics and artificial intelligence through a non-profit endeavor.

Our Product

What is IntellAgent?

  • The App

    Our app uses a proprietary machine learning algorithm to help people with hearing impairments understand lectures in their class. Use our simple UI to record and "listen" to a lecture by transcribing the audio into visual sign language on the screen!

    Learn About IntellAgent

  • Reception

    For those who may not own an iPhone, we have a cheap hardware device that replicates our application! Just record the audio and watch the lecturer become your translator.
