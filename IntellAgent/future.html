Safety

Our Greatest Concern

    In keeping Isaac Asimov's "Three Laws of Robotics" in mind, we ensured that our product always acted in the best interests of the user. These laws include:

1. A robot may not injure a human being or, through inaction, allow a human being to come to harm.

2. A robot must obey orders given to it by human beings without conflicting with the First Law.

3. A robot must protect its own existence without conflicting with the First or Second Law.

    Since our product is published on the AppStore, our code is approved by Apple's standards, ensuring a pure, harmless, and safe application. Our hardware device contains the same exact software as our iPhone application, so you can rest assured that it still follows Apple's guideline.
